Stage 1: Recruitment

Volunteers will be recruited from around the world to be trained in the Jazz Hands Forever™ methodology.

Stage 2: Training

Recruits will be put through a 2 week training program where they will master the Jazz Hands Forever™ methodology.

Stage 3: Bussed to Rotation

Upon graduating the training program, recruits are bussed to the border to begin their 4 week rotation.
